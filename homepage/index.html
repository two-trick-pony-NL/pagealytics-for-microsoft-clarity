Frequently Asked Questions

What is Pagelytics and how does it work?

Pagelytics is an iOS app that connects to Microsoft Clarity, enabling you to view your site's analytics directly on your iPhone. It pulls data from Clarity using your device's calls, and doesn't store any of your personal information. We don’t even have a server!

Is Pagelytics free?

Pagelytics is available on the App Store for a one-time purchase of just €0.99. With this, you gain lifetime access to all its features!

Does Pagelytics collect any personal data?

No, Pagelytics does not collect any personal data. We only use the calls made from your device to retrieve data from Microsoft Clarity. We do not store, process, or have access to any of your user data.

Is Pagelytics affiliated with Microsoft?

No, Pagelytics is not affiliated with Microsoft. We are an independent app that uses the public Microsoft Clarity API to provide iOS users with quick access to their website analytics.

Can I use Pagelytics for multiple Clarity accounts?

Currently, Pagelytics supports one Clarity account per device. We are working on enabling multi-account support in a future update!

How can I get support for Pagelytics?

For any issues or questions, feel free to contact us through the support options available in the app. We are happy to help and provide assistance where needed!
