Frequently asked questions

How does Voice Mate work?

Voice Mate simplifies your communication by managing your calls and providing concise summaries. Behind the scenes we create and assign you a unique phone number linked to our AI, giving you maximum flexibility to choose how to route your calls through it.

Is Voice Mate available for both iOS and Android?

Absolutely! Voice Mate caters to both iOS and Android users, ensuring that you can enjoy its convenience regardless of your device preference. Download now

What languages does Voice Mate support?

Voice Mate speaks your language! With support for multiple languages, including English, Spanish, French, German, Italian and Dutch, you can communicate effortlessly in your preferred language. You specify the desired language during signup.

How do I cancel my account, or reset my password?

If you want to cancel your Voice Mate membership simply open the app and tap 'Delete account' on the profile screen. If you forgot your password then you can simply reset your password here.

Can Voice Mate handle multiple calls simultaneously?

Yes! Voice Mate efficiently manages multiple calls, ensuring that you never miss an opportunity to connect, allowing you to stay productive and responsive.

Is Voice Mate compatible with all phone carriers?

Voice Mate seamlessly integrates with most major phone carriers. While the process of call forwarding varies between providers, if your carrier supports call forwarding, Voice Mate should work smoothly with it. Learn more about call forwarding in our documentation page.

Can I customize Voice Mate's responses or greetings?

Not yet! We have it on our roadmap to offer custom greetings and responses, but this will not be available on launch.

How secure is Voice Mate in handling my calls and data?

Voice Mate employs advanced encryption measures to safeguard your calls and personal data. We will never share or sell your data. To process your calls we work with trusted third parties, like telecommunication and AI providers. In such cases we share as little as possible and only for as long as it takes to process the call. See how we handle your data in our privacy statement
